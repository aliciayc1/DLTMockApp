    • About Us

    Division 30 South is one of many divisions under the California-Nevada-Hawaii (CNH) District.

    There are currently 13 high schools making up our division in total- Anaheim, Cypress, Fairmont, JFK, Katella, Los Alamitos, Loara, Magnolia, Oxford, Savanna, Servite, Western, and Whitney.

    As Key Club members, we strive to demonstrate leadership among our community. Being a student-led organization, we provide service as well as spirit-building opportunities that train high school students to be the best leaders they can be.

    Our core values- caring, character-building, inclusiveness, and leadership- guide us in our service and are what we aim to teach others.

    Have any questions? Contact our LTG at d30s.ltg@cnhkeyclub.org
